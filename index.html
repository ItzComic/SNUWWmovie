THE LEGEND

Origins: The Second Salem

FILLER TEXT: The town of Whitewater, Wisconsin, has long been shrouded in mystery. Local folklore speaks of a "Second Salem," a place where dark forces are said to have taken root. The earliest recorded incident dates back to 1933 when three children disappeared from the Miller Estate, only to be found days later in Whitewater Woods with no memory of their ordeal. This event marked the beginning of a series of unexplained phenomena that would plague the area for decades.

The Phantom

FILLER TEXT: Reported sightings began in 2018. Witnesses describe a figure moving with inhuman speed along the local trails, particularly near Starin Park and the Miller Memorial Grounds. The entity does not appear in daylight. The figure's features remain obscured, though accounts mention "white cloth" and "an absence where the eyes should be."

Pattern Recognition
