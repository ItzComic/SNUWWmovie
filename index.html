THE LEGEND

Origins: The Second Salem

Whitewater has always harbored darkness beneath its suburban facade. In the 1930s, the town earned a sinister reputation as the "Second Salem"—a place where strange accidents befell outsiders and whispers of occult activity lingered in the Miller family estates.

The Phantom

Reported sightings began in 2018. Witnesses describe a figure moving with inhuman speed along the local trails, particularly near Starin Park and the Miller Memorial Grounds. The entity does not appear in daylight. The figure's features remain obscured, though accounts mention "white cloth" and "an absence where the eyes should be."

Pattern Recognition

Incidents cluster around autumn equinox and summer solstice. Campus security reports an uptick in student disappearances during these periods. The common factor: all missing students were last seen near the trails. All exhibits signs of acute psychological distress before vanishing.
